# Update Report - v1.0

## Summary

Version 1.0 is the live YUKA baseline before the History update. It includes Scanner, Explore, Product Details, expanded nutrition evidence, and alternative product cards. It does not include a History page.

## Baseline Screens

### Screen: Scanner

![Scanner](assets/screens/v1.0/scanner.png)

Scanner is the baseline entry point for scanning or searching products.

### Screen: Explore

![Explore](assets/screens/v1.0/explore.png)

Explore is the baseline browsing page with category rows, product images, brands, and nutrition scores.

### Screen: Product Details

![Product Details](assets/screens/v1.0/product-detail.png)

Product Details is the baseline shared detail page for products opened from scan, search, Explore, and Alternatives.

## Version Note

History is absent in v1.0. The v1.1 update changes that single missing page by adding Product History as a top-level section.
