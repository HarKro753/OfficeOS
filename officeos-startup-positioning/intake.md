# Intake: OfficeOS
*Skill: startup-positioning | Generated: 2026-06-10*

## Product Hypothesis
OfficeOS is a managed mobile app creation and update service for non-technical app owners. Customers describe what they want, OfficeOS turns it into a scoped app baseline, implements it, connects core infrastructure, and keeps future updates enforceable through the same dashboard workflow.

## Starting Tension
The prior wedge positioned OfficeOS around human-verified updates for existing apps. The founder now sees a structural weakness: maintaining arbitrary existing apps requires repo access, architecture discovery, signing access, infrastructure compatibility, and trust before OfficeOS has delivered value. The stronger thesis is that OfficeOS should create the app baseline first, then sell maintenance and updates on top of a controlled foundation.

## Best-Fit Customer
Non-technical founders, creators, coaches, local businesses, and small operators who want a real mobile app but do not want to hire, brief, and manage a developer every time they need a build or update.

## Primary Alternatives
- Vibe coding or AI app builders.
- No-code app builders.
- Freelancers.
- Agencies.
- Doing nothing or keeping the app idea in notes/Figma.
- For existing apps: paying the original developer or trying to onboard a new one.
