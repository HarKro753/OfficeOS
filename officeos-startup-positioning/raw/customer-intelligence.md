## Best-Fit Customer Profile
The best first customer is not "any app owner." It is a non-technical person with a narrow, monetizable app idea and enough urgency to pay for a controlled first release: a coach, creator, local service business, niche community operator, or solo founder. They care more if the app needs payments, onboarding, content, booking, membership, or simple recurring updates.

## Switching Triggers
- They tried an AI/no-code tool and got stuck at production details.
- They received agency quotes that feel too large for a first version.
- They need payments, App Store readiness, or a small app shipped for a campaign.
- They had a previous developer experience that left them dependent.
