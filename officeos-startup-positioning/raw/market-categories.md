## Recommendation
Use **managed app creation service** as the category label. The sharper phrasing for copy is: **"managed mobile app creation"**.

This category lets OfficeOS compete against agencies and freelancers on outcome, while avoiding the AI-builder trap. The differentiator is not "AI writes code." The differentiator is that the app is created inside a system designed for future updates: spec, acceptance criteria, infrastructure baseline, QA, release package, and maintenance plan.
